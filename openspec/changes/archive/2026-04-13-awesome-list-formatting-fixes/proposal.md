## Why

The awesome-openspec list does not fully comply with the [sindresorhus/awesome list guidelines](https://github.com/sindresorhus/awesome/blob/main/pull_request_template.md). Fixing these formatting issues is required for inclusion in the awesome list ecosystem and improves consistency for contributors.

## What Changes

- Fix "UI's" to "UIs" (no apostrophe for plurals) in TOC and section header
- Alphabetically sort entries within each section
- Fix extra space in video entry (`-  I` → `- I`)
- Title-case section header: "OpenSpec as integration or plugin" → "OpenSpec as Integration or Plugin"
- Give distinct names to two video entries both called "OpenSpec Video"
- Update CONTRIBUTING.md to say "add in alphabetical order" instead of "add at end of section"

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities

- `formatting-compliance`: Ensure README.md formatting matches awesome list guidelines (periods, spacing, casing).
- `alphabetical-ordering`: Sort all entries within each section alphabetically.
- `contributing-guidelines`: Update CONTRIBUTING.md to reflect actual ordering convention.
